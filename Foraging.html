Foraging

Foraging is one of the main skills you can level up. Foraging experiance is gaing by breaking trees.

This is one of the least enjoyable skills as it is very slow and you can get the best equipment for it very quickly

Armor

There is no specific armor for foraging

Additional Information

This skill sucks to level up and it takes forever. You can get the best gear in the game for about a million coins(which is not a lot).

Axes

This is the Treecapitator and it is the best axe in the game. When you break a log it will destroy 35 additional logs connected to the first log on a 2 second cooldown.

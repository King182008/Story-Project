Farming

Farming is one of the main skills you can level up. Farming experiance is gained by farming crops.

All of the crops in the game include: Wheat, Potatos, Carrots, Pumpkins, Melons, Sugercane, Mushroom, Cactus, Cocoa Beans, and Nether Wart

Armor

This is the best farming armor. It gives lots farming fortune

Additional Information

Farming Fortune will mutliple how many crops you get when you harvest them. Everytime you pass 100 farming fotune you have have a chance to get an extra crop

0 farming fortune is a single crop

100 farming fortune is 2 crops

200 farming fortune is 3 crops

etc...

Tools

While farming you use an asortment of tools specific to each crop however each tool will either be a hoe or an axe

Mining

Mining is one of the main skills you can level up. Mining experiance is gained by mining ores and other blocks.

All the ores include: The basic minecraft ores, mitheral, gemstones, hardstone, glacite, tungston, and umber.

Armor

This is the best mining armor. It gives lots of mining fortune. It also has 5 gemstone slots

Additional Information

Mining Fortune will mutliple how many ores you get when you mine them. Everytime you pass 100 mining fortune you have have a chance to get an extra ore

0 mining fortune is a single ore

100 mining fortune is 2 ore

200 mining fortune is 3 ore

etc...

When you get to a high enough level you'll be able to mine gemstone which can be applied to gear to give them extra stats

Tools
